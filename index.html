Remove Background from Photos

Our AI background remover analyzes your image to detect the subject and separate it from the background. The result is a transparent PNG that you can place on any background — white, colored, or custom.

Best for: Product photos for e-commerce, profile pictures, social media posts, presentations, and marketing materials. Works on people, objects, animals, and complex subjects.

How it works: The tool samples the border pixels of your image to detect the dominant background color, then removes pixels that match using an intelligent threshold algorithm with smooth edge transitions.

Restore Old & Damaged Photos

Transform faded, scratched, or low-quality old photographs into clear, vivid memories. Our AI Photo Restore tool enhances detail, corrects color balance, and improves overall sharpness without destroying the original character of the photo.

Best for: Family archive photos, historical images, black-and-white film photos, and any photo that has degraded over time due to age or storage conditions.

Combine with: Use Colorize Old Photo to add natural color to black-and-white images after restoration for the most impressive results.

Pro Tips for Better Results

💡

Background Removal

For best results, use photos with a solid, single-color background (white, blue, green). High contrast between subject and background produces the cleanest edges.

💡

Image Enhancement

Stack tools for dramatic improvements: start with Noise Remover, then apply Photo Enhancer, and finish with Image Sharpener for professional-grade results.

💡

Portrait Retouching

Use Skin Smoothing first, then Brightness & Contrast to add warmth, and Eye Enhancer last. Keep adjustments subtle — 10-20% changes look most natural.

💡

File Format Tips

Use PNG for images with transparency or text. Use WEBP for web use (50% smaller than JPG). Use JPG for photos that don't need a transparent background.

More Frequently Asked Questions

How large can my image file be?

Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device memory. Most modern devices handle files up to 20MB comfortably. For very large files, use Image Compressor first.

Can I use ImageBoostAI offline?

Once the page loads, most tools work offline since processing is local. You need an internet connection only for the initial page load and to check for updates.

Is ImageBoostAI free forever?

Yes. All 35 tools are permanently free. The service is supported by non-intrusive Google AdSense advertising. We do not plan to introduce mandatory paid plans.

What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. Mobile browsers on iOS and Android are fully supported. We recommend Chrome for the best performance with large images.

Can I edit RAW camera files?

RAW files (CR2, NEF, ARW) are not supported as browsers cannot decode them natively. Convert your RAW file to JPEG or PNG first using your camera's software, then upload here.

Will the results have a watermark?

Never. All downloaded results are completely clean with no watermarks, no ImageBoostAI branding overlaid, and full resolution. What you edit is exactly what you download.
