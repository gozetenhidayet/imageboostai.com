Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: June 28, 2025

🔒 Short version: We don't sell your data. Files you upload are automatically deleted after 1 hour. We use cookies for analytics and advertising. You can opt out anytime.

1. Who We Are

ImageBoostAI ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website imageboostai.com. We provide AI-powered photo editing, PDF tools, and other digital tools. For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@imageboostai.com

2. Information We Collect

Files you upload: When you use our tools, you may upload images, PDFs, or other files. These files are processed by our AI and automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour of upload.

Usage data: We automatically collect certain information when you visit our site:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Country/region (not precise location)

Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for analytics and advertising purposes (see Section 6).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI tools and services
  • To analyze how our website is used (analytics)
  • To show relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We never: sell your personal data, use your uploaded files to train our AI models, or share your data with third parties for their own marketing.

4. File Handling & Security

All files uploaded to ImageBoostAI are:

  • Transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Processed by our AI models in isolated environments
  • Automatically deleted within 1 hour of upload
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never used to train our AI models

⚠️ Please do not upload files containing sensitive personal information such as government IDs, medical records, or financial documents.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate interests: Providing and improving our services, security, fraud prevention
  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and advertising (you can withdraw consent anytime)
  • Legal obligation: Where required by applicable law

6. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

7. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about your visit including pages viewed, time on site, and general location. We have enabled IP anonymization. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Data Retention

  • Uploaded files: Deleted within 1 hour
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Server logs: Retained for 30 days for security purposes
  • Cookies: As specified by each cookie (see cookie policy)

9. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Opt-out of sale (CCPA): We do not sell personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@imageboostai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, to protect your data when transferred internationally.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
